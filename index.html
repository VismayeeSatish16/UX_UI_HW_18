A little bit about Myself

My name is Vismayee Satish and I'm a UI/UX designer who takes pride in crafting visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that enhance productivity. My primary focus is collaborating with clients to develop innovative and impactful UI designs that maximize their product's potential.

In addition to my design work, I have over three years of experience counselling and providing yoga therapy to children with special needs at the Vydehi Institute of Medical Science and Research Centre in Bangalore, India. For the past six years, I have also been freelancing as an online yoga instructor and counsellor. Furthermore, I have been serving as an educator in Kindergarten services for the past three years in Australia.
